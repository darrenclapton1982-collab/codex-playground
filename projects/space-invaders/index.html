Project structure

  • app.js wires DOM and bootstraps the game module.
  • src/config.js stores gameplay tuning and shared constants.
  • src/game.js orchestrates state, loop, and lifecycle hooks.
  • src/render.js and src/entities.js handle drawing and entity helpers.

Controls

  • Move: Arrow keys or A / D
  • Fire: Spacebar
  • Pause: P or Esc
